Account Deletion Request

DishWish and its developer, DishWish Inc., respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. If you wish to have your account and any associated data permanently deleted, please follow these steps:

How to Request Deletion

  1. Click the link below to open your email client:
  2. dishwishsupport@gmail.com (Subject: “Account Deletion Request”)
  3. In the email body, please include:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your DishWish username (if different)
  4. Send the email. We will confirm receipt and complete your deletion request within 72 hours.

Data to Be Deleted

  • Deleted: Email address, user profile, recipe history, favorites.
  • Retained (up to 30 days): Anonymized usage analytics and crash reports for troubleshooting.

Additional Retention

Some anonymized logs or records required by law (e.g., payment records) may be retained for up to 2 years. All personal data covered by your request will be fully deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at dishwishsupport@gmail.com.
