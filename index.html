Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

At DishWish, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. Please read it carefully before using the app.

Our app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from that age group.
